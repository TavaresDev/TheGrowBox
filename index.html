The Grow Box

The grow box System works in 3 different levels

  1. Monitor your plant Locally
    • The box has a LCD display that shows important data from your plant like:
    • Moisture of the soil, humidity, temperature and quantity of light
  2. Access data online wherever you are
    • The box has wi-fi connection so you can check how is you plant doing
    • The sensors update every minute
    • All data is recorded so you can analize if you want
  3. Automate the irrigation process
    • The box can water your plant exactly when it needed
    • Based on the readings the box can plum water from the reservoir
    • Your only job is to fill the reservoir when its empty, once a month
