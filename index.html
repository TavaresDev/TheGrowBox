The grow Box project is a way of helping people to grow heath food indoors. even if someone dont have a lot of time or skils in growing anything. The project its a sistem that monitore important arpects of a plant cicle life. Showing to the user in a simple and organized way. So you have a precise way of knowing how is your crop. You can see The humidity of the air, the moisture of the soil, temoerature of the space, and how much ligth the plant is haciving. Those are the most importants parameters in any plant life. For Those with more interesin in deep understand, all the data recorded from the plant and enviroment is avaible online in a precise grafh. Also have auto pilot mode, where you just need to conect the growBox and the water resevoair and the sistem will water the plant exatly when is needed.
